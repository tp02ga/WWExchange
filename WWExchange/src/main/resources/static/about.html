  • Here's a bit about us

    It's quite simple really.

    As a fellow "Commercial Woodworker", I got tired of two things whenever I bought or sold a piece of used woodworking equipment:

    1. Having someone in the middle between buyer and seller.

        and more importantly

    2. Paying Commissions.

    With help from someone I now consider a friend, and certainly unquestionably a gifted programmer, "Woodworking Exchange" was created and launched.

    My sincere hope is that you eventually find "Woodworking Exchange" an indispensable tool whenever it comes to the buying or selling of your used woodworking equipment.

    The listings will be few at first, but if we all work together, we will build something of benefit to us all!
